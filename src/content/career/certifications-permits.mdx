Professional certifications and safety permits demonstrating commitment to industry standards, workplace safety, and continuous professional development.

## Safety Certifications

### H2S & SO2 Entry Permit

**Issuer:** Petroleum Development Oman (PDO)  
**Status:** Active  
**Description:** Authorized entry permit for working in areas with potential exposure to Hydrogen Sulfide (H2S) and Sulfur Dioxide (SO2). Covers hazard recognition, emergency response procedures, and safe work practices in oil and gas environments.

**Key Competencies:**
- H2S gas detection and monitoring
- Emergency response and evacuation procedures
- Use of respiratory protection equipment
- Safe work practices in confined spaces
- Toxic gas exposure prevention

### ISO 45001:2018 - Occupational Health & Safety Management

**Certification Year:** 2024  
**Standard:** ISO 45001:2018  
**Description:** International standard for occupational health and safety management systems, demonstrating knowledge of systematic approaches to improving workplace safety and preventing work-related injuries and illnesses.

**Key Knowledge Areas:**
- Risk assessment and hazard identification
- Safety management system implementation
- Incident investigation and corrective actions
- Legal compliance and regulatory requirements
- Continuous improvement in workplace safety
